What are IM Short Cuts?
IM Short Cuts allow you to instantly add any of our "Communicate" Smileys (found in the "IM Mini's" category) without opening your Smiley Central window.
How do I use IM Short Cuts?
It's easy! Open an IM window and in its text box type "//" (without the quotes) followed by the IM abbreviation.

For example, typing "//LOL" would insert into your IM.
